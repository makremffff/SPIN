Welcome

Big League

Big League is a competitive Telegram app where real players go head to head every season.

Each season has a full leaderboard. The players with the most tickets at the end of the season win real USDT paid directly to their wallet.

No bots. No shortcuts. Just your effort against everyone else.

How It Works

Play to Rank

Every action you take earns you competition tickets. The more tickets you hold by season end, the higher you place on the board.

Your path to the podium
01
Watch Ads Daily
Each ad you watch awards you tickets and adds to your USDT balance. You have a daily limit — use it every day without missing.
02
Complete Social Tasks
Join channels, follow accounts, and submit proof. Verified tasks give you big ticket bonuses on top of your daily ads.
03
Invite Your Network
Every friend you bring in earns you referral tickets automatically. The more you invite, the faster you climb the board.
04
Watch the Leaderboard
Track your rank live against every player in the season. The countdown timer shows you exactly how much time is left to move up.
Your Rewards

Real USDT,
Every Season

Every season ends with a prize payout. The top players on the podium receive USDT sent straight to their wallet — no minimum withdrawal, no waiting list.

Where the money comes from
01
Ads Build Your Balance
Each ad you watch credits a fixed USDT amount to your in-app balance. Watch every day to stack up your earnings before you withdraw.
02
Season Prizes Are Bigger
The top 3 players at season end receive a prize from the season prize pool — paid on top of whatever ad balance they already earned.
03
Withdraw Anytime
Once you hit the minimum, withdraw your USDT balance to any TON wallet at any time. Season prizes are sent automatically after the podium locks in.
Grow Together

Invite &
Multiply

Your referral link is one of the most powerful tools in the game. Every friend who joins through your link earns you tickets and USDT commission automatically — every time they earn.

How referrals work
01
Share Your Link
Tap the Invite button on the home screen to copy your unique referral link or share it directly to any chat.
02
They Join, You Earn
The moment a friend opens the app through your link and starts playing, you receive referral tickets added to your season total instantly.
03
Passive USDT Commission
Every time your referral watches an ad, a percentage of their earnings flows directly into your USDT balance — forever, with no cap.
04
The More, The Better
There's no limit on how many friends you can invite. The bigger your network, the faster your tickets and balance grow — even while you sleep.
The Rules

Play Fair,
Win Real

Big League is built on honest competition. We use server-side verification for every reward. Cheating doesn't just get you banned — it removes you from the prize pool permanently.

What you need to know
01
One Account Per Person
Each Telegram account is allowed one player profile. Creating multiple accounts to farm rewards will result in a permanent ban on all of them.
02
Real Ads, Real Rewards
Every ad view is verified by the ad network before the reward is credited. Closing ads early or using bots resets your streak and voids the reward.
03
Task Proof Is Checked
Social tasks require manual proof reviewed by our team. Submitting fake screenshots or leaving channels after verification will disqualify your account.
04
Prizes Go to Real Players
At season end, the top accounts are reviewed before prizes are sent. Any account flagged for abuse is removed and the prize moves to the next clean player on the board.
